# Adekemi (Kemi) Sijuwade Ukadike

### Artist • Journalist • Creative Technologist • Educator • Accessibility Advocate

I explore the intersection of accessibility, civic information, AI literacy, environmental observation, cultural memory, and speculative storytelling through research, design, technology, and public-interest inquiry.

My work draws from journalism, disability advocacy, artist support, emerging technology, and worldbuilding. Through software, education, writing, and creative practice, I investigate how systems shape the way people learn, communicate, remember, and imagine futures.

---

## Small Systems Lab

**Small Systems Lab (SSL)** is my independent research and creative practice.

Through projects including Omoluabi, Echo, Earth Sensors Lab, Accessible by Design, and Umada, SSL explores accessibility, civic information, AI literacy, environmental observation, cultural memory, and speculative storytelling.
